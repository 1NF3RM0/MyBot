Suggested Improvement Slots (Optional Enhancements)

Before Strategy Selection

Multi-timeframe confirmation → Ensure signals align on short and long-term charts.

Correlation Filter → Avoid trading correlated symbols simultaneously.

Strategy Evaluation

Add additional strategies: ATR breakout, EMA crossovers, ADX trend strength.

Use rolling historical performance to dynamically adjust confidence.

Position Sizing

Weight lot size based on combined confidence scores.

Implement dynamic risk adjustment if equity drawdown occurs.

Monitoring / Exits

Trailing stop-loss based on ATR.

Profit-target exits (e.g., 1.5–2x stake).

Consider time-to-expiry for early exits.

Analytics & Logging

Record win/loss per strategy.

Calculate expectancy, sharpe ratio, and rolling ROI.

Backtest strategies automatically before live trading.
